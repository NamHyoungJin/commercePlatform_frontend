Overall Mood
The site should feel:

밝고 깨끗한
따뜻한 악보 종이 같은
예배팀이 신뢰할 수 있는
너무 가볍지 않은
음악 스튜디오처럼 정돈된
검색과 악보 탐색이 쉬운
프리미엄이지만 친근한
Avoid:

Pure white everywhere
Cold blue corporate look
Heavy black backgrounds
Neon gradients
Purple/blue gradient hero
Generic ecommerce style
Excessive roundness
Decorative blobs or orbs
Header
현재 헤더 구조는 유지한다.

라이트 테마로 변경한다.

Header background: warm white or ivory
Use subtle bottom border
Navigation text: charcoal
Active/hover menu state: teal underline or soft teal background
Logo should sit naturally on the light header
Login link: charcoal ghost style
Register button: teal filled
Dropdowns: white surface, subtle shadow, charcoal text
Hero Section
현재 메인 히어로 구조와 텍스트는 유지한다.

라이트 테마에서는 어두운 영상 위 강한 검정 오버레이 대신, 밝고 따뜻한 음악 스튜디오 느낌으로 변경한다.

Options:

Use the existing video/image but apply a light warm overlay.
If the image is too dark, use a brighter crop or lighter treatment.
Keep text readable with charcoal or deep charcoal.
Hero style:

Background should feel airy and warm
Use ivory / warm paper tone overlay
Title color: charcoal
Subtitle: soft charcoal
Primary CTA: teal filled
Secondary CTA: charcoal text with teal hover
Search bar should be large and prominent
The hero should not become a dark cinematic section. It should become a bright premium landing section.

Search Bar
Search is the main action.

Design:

Large rounded pill
White or warm-white surface
Border: subtle charcoal alpha
Shadow: soft, not heavy
Placeholder: muted gray
Search icon/button: teal
Focus: teal border and very soft teal glow

Typography
Keep text content the same, but make hierarchy consistent.

Suggested scale:

Hero title: 48-64px desktop, 32-40px mobile
Section title: 28-36px desktop, 24-28px mobile
Card title: 15-17px
Metadata: 12-14px
Button text: 14-15px
Use charcoal for main text, not pure black.

Layout Width
Do not change the overall layout structure.

But if content feels too wide, use these max widths:

Visual hero/image areas: full width or up to 1920px
Main content: 1320px
Wide card grid: 1440px
Readable text: 680-760px
Shapes
Use restrained radius:

Small buttons: 6px
Cards: 8px
Large panels: 10-12px
Search pill and badges: fully rounded
Avoid making all cards too rounded.

Shadows
Use soft realistic shadows.

Cards: subtle shadow
Header/dropdowns: soft shadow
Search bar: slightly stronger soft shadow
Avoid heavy black shadows
Accent Rules
Use teal for:

search
primary CTA
active states
links
selected filters
hover borders
Use orange for:

price
purchase emphasis
important numeric highlights
Do not use bright blue as a primary accent.

Final Result
The final homepage should keep the same content and structure, but look like a bright, warm, premium worship score platform.

It should feel like:

“밝은 악보 종이와 따뜻한 예배 스튜디오 감성 위에, 청록색 검색/선택 포인트와 오렌지 구매 포인트가 정돈된 OnlyOneMusic Score 메인페이지.”
